## How to Get Listed

1. **Ship something** (code, research, tools, quality content)
2. **Contribute** to the ecosystem
3. **Be consistent** (not just one viral post)
4. **Submit a PR** to this repo with evidence of your work

**We prioritize:** Code > Research > Thoughtful Analysis > Engagement

**We do NOT list:**
- Token promoters without utility
- Spam accounts
- Known bad actors
